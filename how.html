How Lotty Lottery Works

Lotty is a decentralized lottery running fully on the Solana blockchain, designed for fairness, transparency, and community participation.

  • Ticket Purchase: Players buy Lotty tokens which give them entry into the lottery pool.
  • Draw Schedule: Every 15 minutes, a winner is selected at random from all participants holding tokens.
  • Claim Window: Winners have 1 minute to claim their prize, or the pot is burned to maintain fairness.
  • Stake to Rig It: Players can stake tokens to boost their odds of winning — but the system is designed so the lottery remains unpredictable.
  • Transparency: All transfers and draws are recorded on-chain, so anyone can verify fairness.

Lotty combines blockchain innovation with fun and excitement — join the game and see if luck is on your side!
