How to Buy Lotty Token

Buying Lotty tokens is easy and can be done securely on the Solana blockchain. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Step 1: Set up a Solana wallet such as Phantom or Solflare.
  • Step 2: Fund your wallet with SOL from an exchange or other wallet.
  • Step 3: Connect your wallet to a trusted decentralized exchange (DEX) like Raydium or Orca.
  • Step 4: Search for the Lotty token by its contract address (ask us for the address or find it on our official channels).
  • Step 5: Swap your SOL for Lotty tokens and enjoy participating in the lottery!

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out on our official social channels or support page.
